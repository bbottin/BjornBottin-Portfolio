About

I am an experienced Microsoft SharePoint Specialist and Front-End Web Developer, with a strong background in managing intranet and mobile platforms for leading organizations. At the Abu Dhabi Investment Authority (ADIA) in the UAE, one of the world’s largest financial institutions, I oversaw SharePoint and web development. Prior to this, I managed SharePoint intranet and internet environments for Life Healthcare, one of South Africa's largest private hospital groups.

Born and raised in South Africa, I initially pursued a B.Com in Accounting and Informatics before transitioning into IT, earning my Microsoft Certified Systems Engineer (MCSE) qualification in 1999. During my studies, I gained hands-on experience as a Junior LAN Administrator at Nedbank, contributing to Y2K compliance projects.

My professional journey began in data analytics and intranet development at an insurance company, where I first encountered Microsoft SharePoint. In 2007, I joined Life Healthcare as a SharePoint Administrator and external webmaster, working with SharePoint versions 2007, 2010, and 2013 while developing expertise in web design and coding.

In 2015, I relocated to the UAE to work as a SharePoint Administrator and Front-End Web Developer for ADIA, delivering tailored solutions using SharePoint versions 2010, 2013, and 2016. Since returning to South Africa in 2019, I have operated as a freelance consultant, specializing in Web Development, Microsoft 365, and SharePoint services and various other IT related services.

What I Do

I specialize in front-end web development, focusing on creating visually appealing and functional websites. Additionally, I handle some back-end tasks, including hosting environment setup and configuration.

I also assist organizations in maximizing their use of Microsoft SharePoint as an intranet platform. This includes server and SharePoint environment setup, consulting with stakeholders, and implementing both out-of-the-box features and custom solutions tailored to business needs.

With over 24 years of IT experience, my expertise spans building and upgrading computers, LAN network administration, software and hardware troubleshooting, and setting up home and small business networks. For a detailed overview of my current services, please refer to the full service list.
